  <div class="content-section">
    <h2>Product Overview</h2>
    <p>
      MASTER CHEM 1008 is an unsanded cementitious grout ideal for narrow joints up to 3mm. It provides smooth filling, excellent adhesion, and a neat, uniform finish for interior tile installations.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="content-section">
    <h2>Key Features</h2>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Smooth Finish:</strong> Fine texture perfect for narrow grout joints</li>
      <li><strong>Excellent Adhesion:</strong> Strong bonding to tile edges</li>
      <li><strong>Easy Application:</strong> Flows smoothly into narrow joints</li>
      <li><strong>Non-Shrinking:</strong> Maintains joint integrity after curing</li>
      <li><strong>Color Consistency:</strong> Uniform color throughout the joint</li>
      <li><strong>Stain Resistant:</strong> Resists common household stains</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="content-section">
    <h2>Applications</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Ceramic and porcelain tiles with joints up to 3mm</li>
      <li>Wall tile installations in bathrooms and kitchens</li>
      <li>Polished marble and granite with narrow joints</li>
      <li>Rectified tiles requiring minimal joint width</li>
      <li>Interior residential and commercial spaces</li>
      <li>Decorative tile patterns with fine joints</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="content-section">
    <h2>Technical Specifications</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Joint width: Up to 3mm</li>
      <li>Coverage: Approximately 1-1.5 kg/m² (depending on joint size)</li>
      <li>Mixing ratio: As per manufacturer guidelines</li>
      <li>Working time: 30-40 minutes</li>
      <li>Initial setting: 2-3 hours</li>
      <li>Final curing: 24-48 hours</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="content-section">
    <h2>Application Guidelines</h2>
    <p>
      Ensure tiles are properly set and adhesive is cured before grouting. Mix grout with clean water to achieve smooth, lump-free consistency. Apply diagonally across joints using a rubber float. Remove excess immediately with damp sponge. Polish with dry cloth after initial setting. Avoid water contact for 24 hours. Contact our technical team for detailed application instructions.
    </p>
  </div>
