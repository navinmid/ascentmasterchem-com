    <h2>Product Overview</h2>
    <p>
      MASTER CHEM 1017 is a standard-grade cementitious grout suitable for general-purpose tile joints. It ensures strong bonding, consistent color, and durability for both wall and floor applications.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="content-section">
    <h2>Key Features</h2>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Versatile Application:</strong> Suitable for both walls and floors</li>
      <li><strong>Consistent Color:</strong> Uniform color throughout the installation</li>
      <li><strong>Strong Bonding:</strong> Reliable adhesion to tile edges</li>
      <li><strong>Easy to Mix:</strong> Simple preparation for quick application</li>
      <li><strong>Cost Effective:</strong> Economical solution for standard projects</li>
      <li><strong>Good Coverage:</strong> Efficient material usage</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="content-section">
    <h2>Applications</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>General-purpose wall and floor tiling</li>
      <li>Residential bathrooms and kitchens</li>
      <li>Commercial interior spaces</li>
      <li>Ceramic and porcelain tile joints (3-10mm)</li>
      <li>Standard traffic areas</li>
      <li>Interior dry and semi-wet areas</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="content-section">
    <h2>Technical Specifications</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Joint width: 3mm to 10mm</li>
      <li>Coverage: Approximately 1.5-2 kg/m² (depending on joint size)</li>
      <li>Mixing ratio: As per manufacturer guidelines</li>
      <li>Working time: 30-40 minutes</li>
      <li>Initial setting: 3-4 hours</li>
      <li>Final curing: 24-48 hours</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="content-section">
    <h2>Application Guidelines</h2>
    <p>
      Allow tile adhesive to cure fully before grouting (minimum 24 hours). Clean joints of any debris or adhesive residue. Mix grout with clean water to a smooth, workable consistency. Apply using rubber float, forcing grout into joints. Remove excess with damp sponge using circular motions. Buff with dry cloth after initial set. Contact our technical team for detailed application instructions.
    </p>
  </div>
