  <div class="content-section">
    <h2>Product Overview</h2>
    <p>
      MASTER CHEM 1026 is specially formulated for large-format tiles and natural stone installations. It offers superior grip, prevents slippage, and ensures a uniform, professional finish for demanding projects.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="content-section">
    <h2>Key Features</h2>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Superior Grip:</strong> Exceptional bonding for large-format tiles and heavy stone</li>
      <li><strong>Anti-Slip Formula:</strong> Prevents tile slippage during installation</li>
      <li><strong>Extended Open Time:</strong> Adequate working time for precise placement</li>
      <li><strong>High Load Bearing:</strong> Suitable for heavy tiles and natural stone</li>
      <li><strong>Minimal Sagging:</strong> Maintains position on vertical surfaces</li>
      <li><strong>Uniform Coverage:</strong> Ensures professional, consistent finish</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="content-section">
    <h2>Applications</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Large-format porcelain tiles (600mm x 1200mm and above)</li>
      <li>Natural stone installations (marble, granite, limestone)</li>
      <li>Heavy ceramic tiles on walls and floors</li>
      <li>Commercial building facades and lobbies</li>
      <li>High-end residential projects</li>
      <li>Swimming pool surrounds and wet areas</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="content-section">
    <h2>Technical Specifications</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Suitable for tiles up to 1200mm x 2400mm</li>
      <li>Coverage: Approximately 5-6 kg/m² (at 10mm notch)</li>
      <li>Open time: 30-40 minutes</li>
      <li>Pot life: 4-5 hours</li>
      <li>Setting time: 24-48 hours</li>
      <li>Grouting time: After 48 hours</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="content-section">
    <h2>Application Guidelines</h2>
    <p>
      Surface must be clean, level, and structurally sound. Use a large-notch trowel (10-12mm) for proper coverage. Apply adhesive to both substrate and tile back (back-buttering) for large-format tiles. Ensure full bedding with no voids. Use tile leveling systems for optimal results. Contact our technical team for detailed application instructions.
    </p>
  </div>
